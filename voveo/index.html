Voveo

Simple & secure voting

 

Product

AGM & assemblies

Voveo is a blockchain based voting tool for annual general meetings (AGM) and other types of assemblies or meeting.

Quick & Simple

Voveo runs on any device: desktop, tablet and mobile phones (iOS & Android).

Secure

All polling is stored on the Ethereum blockchain - one of the most secure and immutable database.
